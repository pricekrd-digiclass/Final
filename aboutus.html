Our Story

In the spring of 2015, Klynn's Froyo Shop opened its first frozen yogurt cafe in a small strip mall near the University of Indianapolis just south of downtown Indianapolis, Indiana. With our award winning Original cake flavored froyo recipe, we introduced the no-weight froyo idea to the city. We had overwhelming support for our product from the community, so we opened a few more stores. Now with over 50 locations, Klynn's Froyo Shop is the number one frozen yogurt establishment in the state!

Come in & enjoy one of our fifty rotating flavors. Your tastebuds will thank you!
